Not every Alloco product is available to every user. Eligibility depends on your age, jurisdiction, investor classification, and ability to satisfy identity verification requirements. Before you attempt to access any product, Alloco enforces a combination of KYC checks, investor classification reviews, and jurisdictional controls to determine whether you qualify. Requirements vary by product — some are open to general retail users while others are restricted to accredited, professional, or institutional investors.

## General requirements

To access any Alloco product you must meet all of the following baseline conditions:

- **Age** — you must be at least 18 years old (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction, if higher)
- **Legal capacity** — you must have the legal capacity to enter into financial contracts under the laws of your jurisdiction
- **Lawful use** — accessing Alloco products must be lawful in your country or territory of residence; it is your responsibility to confirm this before proceeding
- **Sanctions compliance** — you must not be subject to any applicable international or domestic sanctions, export controls, or financial crime restrictions
- **KYC/AML** — you must successfully complete Alloco's identity verification and anti-money laundering checks before accessing products
- **Honest representation** — you must not misrepresent your identity, location, investor status, or eligibility at any point during onboarding or ongoing product access

## Product-specific eligibility

Beyond the general baseline, individual products may impose additional eligibility requirements based on their regulatory classification and target investor profile.

Some products are available only to:

- **Accredited investors** — individuals or entities meeting minimum income, net worth, or professional criteria as defined by applicable law
- **Professional investors** — persons with the investment experience, knowledge, and financial means to understand and bear the risks of complex products
- **Qualified investors** — a classification used in certain jurisdictions that may combine elements of accredited and professional investor tests
- **Institutional investors** — funds, banks, asset managers, family offices, and other regulated entities

You must check the individual product prospectus and offering documents before attempting to subscribe or trade. Meeting the general requirements does not automatically mean you qualify for every product on the platform. If a product you are trying to access has investor classification requirements that you do not meet, you will not be permitted to proceed regardless of your KYC status.

## Identity verification (KYC)

All users must complete identity verification before accessing Alloco products. Alloco uses third-party KYC and AML service providers to conduct these checks. You will typically be asked to provide:

- **Full legal name** — exactly as it appears on your government-issued identification
- **Date of birth**
- **Government-issued photo ID** — such as a passport, national identity card, or driver's licence
- **Proof of residential address** — such as a utility bill, bank statement, or government correspondence dated within the past three months
- **Source of funds** — required for certain product types or transaction sizes to satisfy AML obligations
- **Investor classification documentation** — evidence of your accredited, professional, or institutional status where a product requires it

<Info>
  KYC verification is conducted by an independent third-party provider, not directly by Alloco. Alloco does not store your raw identity documents beyond what is required by law and its privacy policy.
</Info>

Completing verification does not guarantee access to any specific product. Alloco and individual product issuers retain the right to decline or revoke access based on risk, compliance, or eligibility grounds, even after successful KYC completion.

## Restricted jurisdictions

Alloco products are not available in all jurisdictions. Alloco uses a combination of IP geolocation, wallet screening, and blockchain analytics tools to detect and enforce geographic restrictions. If you connect from a restricted location, or if your wallet address is associated with a restricted jurisdiction, your access will be blocked or limited.

Restricted jurisdictions may include countries subject to international sanctions, locations where offering Alloco products would require regulatory approvals not currently held, and territories where local law prohibits or restricts access to this type of financial product.

<Warning>
  Do not attempt to circumvent geographic restrictions using VPNs, proxy services, or any other method. Attempting to bypass restrictions may result in permanent suspension of your account, forfeiture of any pending transactions, and referral to relevant authorities where required by law.
</Warning>

The list of restricted jurisdictions may change without notice as regulatory requirements evolve. You are responsible for confirming that your use of Alloco remains lawful at all times.

## Wallet requirements

You need a compatible wallet to interact with Alloco products. Alloco supports both self-custodial wallets and embedded wallets depending on the product and access method.

- **Compatible wallet** — use a wallet that supports the relevant blockchain network(s) on which Alloco products are issued; check the specific product page for supported networks
- **Self-custody responsibility** — if you use a self-custodial wallet, you are solely responsible for safeguarding your private keys and seed phrase; losing access to your keys means losing access to your funds
- **Embedded wallets** — if you use an embedded or managed wallet provided through the Alloco app, review the custody and key management disclosures that apply to that wallet type
- **Wallet screening** — your wallet address may be screened for sanctions exposure, association with illicit activity, or prior flagging by blockchain analytics tools; screened-out wallets will not be permitted to access products

<Warning>
  Never share your private keys or seed phrase with anyone — including Alloco, anyone claiming to represent Alloco, or any third-party service purporting to help you recover access to your wallet. Alloco will **never** ask for your private keys or seed phrase under any circumstances. Anyone who does is attempting to steal your funds.
</Warning>
