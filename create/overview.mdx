Alloco's infrastructure lets anyone with a market insight — from individual traders to institutional asset managers — propose and launch a compliant, investable onchain ETP. Whether you've identified a macro trend, a quantitative strategy, or a professional investment thesis, this page explains how to bring that idea to market.

## Who can create an ETP

Alloco is open to a wide range of creators. You can submit a product idea if you are:

- An individual investor with a clear market insight or investment thesis
- A professional portfolio manager with a documented strategy
- A research team or analyst community with a structured view
- An asset manager looking to bring a fund strategy onchain
- An industry expert with domain knowledge in a specific sector or theme
- A partner institution seeking to co-issue or distribute structured products

## The creation pathway

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit your idea">
    Prepare a submission with your market thesis, target asset universe, strategy rules, and any relevant performance history. Email support@allocofi.com or use the form at app.allocofi.com.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Alloco reviews for compliance and feasibility">
    Alloco's team evaluates your idea for legal compliance, product feasibility, and structural fit on the platform. They will follow up with questions or next steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Product structure is defined">
    Together, you and Alloco define the product parameters: name, ticker, objective, eligible users, fees, rebalancing rules, valuation methodology, and redemption constraints.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Legal wrapper and disclosure layer are set up">
    The appropriate legal structure — such as an SPV, SPC, or segregated portfolio — is established. A prospectus or offering memorandum, risk disclosures, NAV methodology document, and fee schedule are prepared.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Product launched and made accessible">
    Once all documentation is complete and compliance checks pass, the product is listed and made accessible through Alloco's liquidity and distribution infrastructure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Alloco provides — and what you provide

Alloco supplies the full technical and operational stack. As the creator, you supply the market insight and strategy definition.

| Layer | Provided by Alloco | Provided by you |
|---|---|---|
| Product Engine | ✓ Name, ticker, rules, fee templates | ✓ Strategy concept and parameters |
| NAV System | ✓ Official NAV, iNAV, Risk NAV | ✓ Valuation source preference |
| Liquidity Routing | ✓ DEX, RFQ, MM, AP, broker API | ✓ Target investor audience |
| Legal Wrapper | ✓ SPV/SPC/segregated portfolio structure | ✓ Disclosure of relevant rights |
| Disclosure Layer | ✓ Terms, fees, risks, holdings, reports | ✓ Performance history (if applicable) |

<Note>
  Submitting an idea does not obligate Alloco to launch, review, finance, distribute, or maintain a product. Any commercial rights, fees, revenue-sharing, or licensing arrangements must be formalized in a separate written agreement. Contact support@allocofi.com to get started.
</Note>
