Alloco extends beyond public markets to bring select private market opportunities onchain. Whether you are looking to participate in venture-backed companies across the growth lifecycle or access a fund manager's independently branded investment product, Alloco's infrastructure provides the structure, transparency, and onchain access rails that private markets have traditionally lacked. Each product is issued with defined rules, documented risk disclosures, and a custody overview so you know exactly what you are investing in.

## PE & VC access

Private equity and venture capital products on Alloco give you the ability to participate in curated opportunities across the full company growth lifecycle — from early-stage venture rounds to growth equity and later-stage private transactions.

- **Curated deal flow** — each opportunity is selected and structured through Alloco's product infrastructure, with documented terms, eligible investor criteria, and risk disclosures
- **Onchain structure** — your economic interest is recorded onchain, providing transparency over your position, the product's holdings, and any material events during the lifecycle of the investment
- **Structured access** — subscription, redemption, and reporting processes are defined upfront in the product documents, so you understand the timeline and mechanics before you commit capital

Private market products may cover a range of asset types including direct company stakes, fund interests, co-investment vehicles, or SPV-wrapped positions depending on the specific opportunity.

## Manager-led products

Exceptional fund managers can launch independently branded onchain investment products through Alloco's infrastructure. This means a professional manager can define their own strategy, maintain their own brand, and reach a global investor base — while Alloco provides the product issuance engine, NAV system, disclosure layer, and access rails.

Manager-led products benefit from the same structural standards as all Alloco products:

- Published prospectus and risk disclosures
- Defined NAV methodology and reporting cycle
- Onchain share records and custody overview
- DEX, RFQ, or primary market access routes where applicable

If you are a fund manager interested in launching an onchain product, contact Alloco at [support@allocofi.com](mailto:support@allocofi.com).

## Who can access

Private market products on Alloco are typically limited to **professional, accredited, or institutional investors** in line with the applicable regulatory requirements for the product and jurisdiction. Before you can invest, you will need to:

- Complete KYC and AML verification
- Pass eligibility screening based on the product's investor qualification criteria (which may include net worth, investment experience, or professional status requirements)
- Accept the product's terms, prospectus, and risk disclosures

Eligibility requirements vary by product. Review the individual product documentation to understand the specific requirements that apply to you. Access may also be restricted by jurisdiction.
