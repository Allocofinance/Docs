The goal is simple: make complex markets understandable, valuable, and accessible to you without compromising on disclosure or structure.

## What every Alloco product includes

Every product issued on the Alloco platform is built to a shared standard so you always know what you are getting:

- **Defined rules and strategy** — each product has a published strategy description, eligible assets, rebalancing schedule, and fee structure
- **Transparent holdings** — you can review what the product holds, how positions are weighted, and when the last rebalance occurred
- **NAV methodology** — Official NAV, iNAV, and Risk NAV are calculated and published according to a documented methodology
- **Risk disclosures** — material risks, assumptions, and product limitations are disclosed before you invest
- **Custody structure** — safekeeping arrangements, asset segregation, and operational controls are explained in the product's custody overview
- **DEX, RFQ, and primary market access** — you can trade via a decentralised exchange for small orders, request-for-quote routes for medium orders, or the primary creation and redemption market for large orders

## Product objectives

Every Alloco product is designed around six core objectives that define what a product must deliver to you.

| Objective | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Understandable** | Clear narrative, underlying asset or strategy description, target exposure, and risk boundary. |
| **Valuable** | Defined NAV methodology, data sources, update frequency, and exception-handling rules. |
| **Tradable** | Accessible through DEX, RFQ, wallet, exchange, aggregator, or other distribution channel. |
| **Redeemable** | Eligible users, authorised participants, market makers, or institutions may create and redeem through primary market processes. |
| **Protected** | Risk-state logic, NAV staleness checks, premium/discount monitoring, redemption limits, and suspension mechanisms. |
| **Disclosed** | You can review terms, fees, risks, NAV, holdings, strategy logic, custody information, and reports at any time. |
