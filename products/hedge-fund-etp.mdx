Hedge Fund ETPs bring institutional-grade fund strategies onchain through a structured, transparent ETP wrapper. Traditionally, access to hedge fund strategies required large minimum investments, lengthy onboarding, and limited visibility into what the fund actually holds. Alloco changes that by codifying a fund's strategy into a set of published rules, wrapping it in an ETP, and making it accessible to a broader range of investors — with NAV-based pricing, weekly settlement, and full disclosure at every step.

## What's inside

Every Hedge Fund ETP translates a fund's investment strategy into a documented, rule-bound product structure:

- **Eligible assets** — the prospectus defines exactly which asset classes, instruments, and markets the strategy may access
- **Rebalancing schedule** — positions are reviewed and adjusted on a defined cycle, typically weekly, in line with the strategy's rules
- **Risk limits** — concentration limits, leverage caps, and risk-budget constraints are set in the product rules and enforced through the NAV and risk-control system
- **NAV methodology** — Official NAV, iNAV, and Risk NAV are calculated according to a published methodology; you can review the valuation logic, data sources, and update frequency before you invest
- **Fee structure** — management fees, creation/redemption fees, and any performance-related costs are disclosed in the product prospectus

## Transparency features

Hedge Fund ETPs are designed so you always know what you are invested in:

- **Visible holdings** — the product's positions and asset weights are published on a regular basis in line with the prospectus disclosure schedule
- **Published NAV** — Official NAV is calculated after each settlement cycle; iNAV updates continuously during active markets to give you a real-time pricing reference
- **Weekly settlement** — the strategy rebalances on a weekly cycle, with settlement records published after each rebalance
- **Risk disclosures** — material risks, strategy assumptions, and product limitations are documented and available for you to review at any time
- **Custody overview** — safekeeping arrangements, asset segregation, and operational controls are explained in the product's custody documentation

## Access routes

You can access Hedge Fund ETPs through three routes:

- **DEX** — buy or sell on a decentralised exchange for smaller orders; market price may differ from NAV
- **RFQ** — request a firm quote for medium-sized orders, priced against iNAV plus spread and execution cost
- **Primary market** — create or redeem units at Official NAV for large orders, subject to KYC, eligibility verification, and product-specific conditions including market hours and liquidity constraints

<Note>

</Note>
