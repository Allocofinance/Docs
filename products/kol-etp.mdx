KOL ETPs let you invest alongside the public portfolios and market signals of Key Opinion Leaders — analysts, traders, and influential market voices who share their positions openly. Instead of manually following a signal or copying a trade yourself, a KOL ETP packages those positions into a structured, rules-based onchain product with transparent holdings, NAV-based pricing, and a defined rebalancing cycle. You get the exposure without the operational overhead.

## What KOL ETPs track

Each KOL ETP is built around a specific set of publicly disclosed positions or signals from a verified market voice:

- **Public portfolio signals** — positions or disclosures that are already in the public domain, mapped into a basket with defined weighting rules
- **Verified market voices** — the signal source is identified, referenced, and documented in the product prospectus
- **Transparent position-tracking** — current holdings, weights, and any pending rebalances are visible to you at all times
- **Rules-based construction** — inclusion criteria, position limits, and rebalancing triggers are defined upfront, so the product behaves predictably

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Public positions are monitored">
    The KOL's publicly disclosed portfolio or market signals are tracked and verified against the sources named in the product prospectus.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The basket is constructed">
    Eligible assets are selected and weighted according to the product's defined rules — including position limits, concentration caps, and any exclusion criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The product is issued onchain">
    The basket is wrapped into an ETP with a published NAV, a product token representing your economic interest, and full disclosure documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You access via your preferred route">
    You can buy or sell through a DEX for smaller orders, via RFQ for medium-sized orders, or through the primary creation and redemption market for larger positions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access and trading

You can access KOL ETPs through three routes depending on your order size:

- **DEX (small orders)** — swap directly on a decentralised exchange; execution is fast and slippage is visible before you confirm
- **RFQ (medium orders)** — request a firm quote from a market maker based on iNAV, execution cost, and current inventory; multiple quotes may be available
- **Primary market (large orders)** — create or redeem units at or close to Official NAV, subject to KYC, whitelist eligibility, and product-specific conditions

KOL ETPs follow a **weekly settlement and rebalance cycle**. The basket is reviewed and adjusted weekly in line with updated public signals and the product's rebalancing rules. Secondary market trading is available continuously, though market price may differ from NAV.

## Risks to understand

Before investing in a KOL ETP, make sure you understand the following risks:

- **Strategy risk** — the KOL's past signal performance does not guarantee future results; market conditions change and strategies that worked previously may not repeat
- **Rebalancing lag** — there is a delay between a signal being published and the product being rebalanced; the basket may not perfectly reflect the most current positions at all times
- **Premium/discount to NAV** — the market price of the ETP token may trade above or below NAV, particularly during periods of low liquidity or high volatility
- **Concentration risk** — some KOL portfolios may be concentrated in a small number of assets or sectors, which can amplify both gains and losses
