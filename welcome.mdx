Financial products remain locked inside brokerage accounts, geographic borders, and legacy infrastructure — accessible only to those with the right connections, the right address, or the right broker. Alloco was built to change that. Alloco is onchain ETP issuance and access infrastructure that converts market narratives, professional insights, investment themes, asset baskets, and quantitative strategies into ETP/ETF-style products with clear rules, transparent pricing, and global reach — no gatekeepers required.

## What is an onchain ETP?

An ETP (Exchange-Traded Product) is a rules-based investment product that tracks an underlying basket, strategy, or theme. You likely know its close relative, the ETF — one of the most transformative inventions in modern finance precisely because it made complex strategies understandable and accessible to ordinary investors.

An **onchain ETP** takes that same concept and builds it on blockchain infrastructure. Instead of living inside a brokerage account at a single institution, the product exists onchain — where it becomes:

- **Transparent** — holdings, rules, fees, and valuations are published and verifiable
- **Globally accessible** — available to eligible users worldwide without borders or broker relationships
- **24/7 tradable** — you can buy or sell at any time, not just during exchange hours
- **Programmable and composable** — onchain assets can interact with wallets, DeFi protocols, and other digital infrastructure

This is not simply tokenization. Tokenization turns assets into digital units. Alloco turns assets into fully structured financial products — complete with NAV methodology, custody, liquidity, redemption, compliance, and disclosure.

## The Alloco difference

Alloco is designed from the ground up to give you the kind of transparency and access that traditional finance has never offered.

- **Transparent rules, pricing, and holdings** — every product publishes its strategy logic, NAV methodology, fee schedule, and holdings so you always know what you own and how it is valued
- **Global access without gatekeepers** — you can access products directly from your wallet without a broker, custodian relationship, or geographic restriction (subject to eligibility and jurisdiction)
- **24/7 tradability** — onchain infrastructure means markets never fully close; you can trade, monitor, and manage your positions at any hour
- **Programmable, composable assets** — ETP tokens exist onchain and can interact with wallets, DeFi protocols, and other digital financial infrastructure — unlocking capabilities that legacy products simply cannot support
- **Structured for trust** — each product comes with a prospectus, risk disclosures, NAV methodology, and custody overview so you have everything you need to make an informed decision

<Note>
  Alloco products are **not** bank deposits, capital-guaranteed instruments, or fixed-income products. Product prices fluctuate, and you may lose part or all of the capital you invest. Always read the full product documentation — including the prospectus, risk disclosures, NAV methodology, and custody overview — before accessing any Alloco product.
</Note>
