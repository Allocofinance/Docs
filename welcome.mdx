Capital markets have never truly gone online. Financial products remain locked inside brokerage accounts, geographic borders, and legacy infrastructure — accessible only to those with the right connections, the right address, or the right broker. Alloco was built to change that. Alloco is onchain ETP issuance and access infrastructure that converts market narratives, professional insights, investment themes, asset baskets, and quantitative strategies into ETP-style products with clear rules, transparent pricing, and global reach — no gatekeepers required.

## What is an onchain ETP?

An ETP (Exchange-Traded Product) is a rules-based investment product that tracks an underlying basket, strategy, or theme. You likely know its close relative, the ETF — one of the most transformative inventions in modern finance precisely because it made complex strategies understandable and accessible to ordinary investors.

An **onchain ETP** takes that same concept and builds it on blockchain infrastructure.

- **Transparent** — holdings, rules, fees, and valuations are published and verifiable
- **Globally accessible** — available to eligible users worldwide without borders or broker relationships
- **24/7 tradable** — you can buy or sell at any time, not just during exchange hours
- **Programmable and composable** — onchain assets can interact with wallets, DeFi protocols, and other digital infrastructure
