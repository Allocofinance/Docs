## Start Investing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your eligibility">
    Review the [eligibility requirements](/eligibility) for your jurisdiction and investor classification. Some products are limited to accredited or qualified investors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Visit [app.allocofi.com](https://app.allocofi.com) and connect a compatible self-custodial or embedded wallet. Complete identity verification (KYC) to unlock product access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse products">
    Explore the [ETP product catalogue](/products/overview) — from KOL signal portfolios and hedge fund wrappers to yield products and thematic baskets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Buy or invest">
    Use the DEX swap for small orders, request a quote (RFQ) for medium orders, or access the primary market for large subscriptions. See the [Trading & Access guide](/trading/buying-and-selling) for details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
